Company Profile

BOI(Brain Ocean International S.L.) is a team composed of senior Chinese business managers, who have been in Spain for more than 20 years, and a number of economic and trade Spanish experts who are highly respected in Spain. The team provide consulting and business agency services for European and Chinese-funded enterprises in connection with various resources and expansion. The team has multiple interdisciplinary masters and doctors, and undertakes business and technical support for various vertical subdivision industries, such as finance, investment, trade, IT technology, and so on.

The company communicates and cooperates closely with government departments of China and Europe, Spanish enterprises and Chinese-funded enterprises, and plays a key role as a bridge for Chinese-funded enterprises to expand their business in Europe. The main business scope includes:

1) Channel expansion: as an agent to sell products of the company, promote and operate products in in the Spain and other country in Europe. Assist enterprises in the planning and organization of various marketing activities, help Chinese-funded enterprises enter the Iberia market, connect channels with local enterprise decision-makers, build marketing networks, and promote and achieve business goals.

2) Enterprise IT Solutionnology solutions: provide office IT facilities services for enterprises in Spian, including network construction, enterprise office software, enterprise cloud infrastructure, security systems, etc.

3) Services for Chinese Enterprise: As an incubator for Chinese-funded enterprises going overseas, it helps enterprises to solve related matters in the early stage of going overseas, provides professional Spanish-Portuguese talent hunting services for enterprises, provides marketing planning for enterprises and assists in implementation, and makes websites and maintenance for enterprises renew, ect.

4) Investment & Insurance：Planning and arranging asset allocation for corporate income and risk matching, formulating pension plans, corporate investment and financing consultants; providing insurance services including company property insurance, employee medical insurance, accident insurance, etc.


BOI reached deep cooperation with Chinese and Spanish companies, departments of government, solventis, MAPFRE, HUAWEI, VIVO, HIKVISION, Riejie Network, ORANGE, XIAOMI, OPPO, HOENERGY, CMI, Camara Madrid, etc.
